How To Use

  • To search a comic by the starting letters of title: type title in search bar and click the search button.
  • To search all comics: simply click search button without putting in the search.
  • To search by exact title or find more filters: click "Toggle More Options" button and more filters will be displayed.
  • To view the first marvel character in the comic in alphabetical order: hover mouse over a result.
  • To load more options after a search: click load more button at bottom, and 10 more options will load onto the page.
  • The More Options section contains the following filters:
    • Search By
      Exact Title or Starting Letters of Title
    • Format
    • Year Released
    • Order By
    • Sort By
